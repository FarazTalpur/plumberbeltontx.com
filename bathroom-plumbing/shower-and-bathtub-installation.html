Frequently Asked Questions

The average cost of shower and bathtub installation ranges from $1,000 to $5,000, depending on the materials, features, and complexity of the installation.

Installation typically takes between one to three days, depending on the type of shower or bathtub and any additional plumbing or structural work required.

Yes, an existing bathtub can generally be converted into a shower with relative ease, though it may require adjustments to plumbing and the addition of features like a showerhead and enclosure.

Permit requirements vary by location, but generally, a plumbing permit is required for installing a new shower or bathtub to ensure that all work complies with local building codes.

Some of the most durable materials for showers and bathtubs include acrylic, fiberglass, cast iron, and porcelain, each offering different benefits in terms of longevity, maintenance, and cost.
